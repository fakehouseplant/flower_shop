Boquets & Arrangements

Daily Arrangements

We offer a small variety of premade arrangements and boquets daily in store. Please stop in to brighten up your day. Each arrangement is hand selected from local seasonal flowers, and arranged in a natural and painterly fashion. We often sell out early! Premade arrangements priced between $75 - $125.

Sympathy Arrangements

Sympathy arrangements are styled similarly to our other arrangments, but offered in more muted or neutral palattes. Please contact us today to discuss what flowers and foliage we have available. Sympathy arrangements start at $125.

Special Order and Holiday Arrangements

Have a special anniversary or hosting a holiday party soon? Come into the store or contact us to discuss your needs. Please note that for large holidays we need 3 days advance notice for day of delivery. Special orders start at $125.
